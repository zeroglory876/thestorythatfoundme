The Story That Found Me

chapter 1 — posted june 22, 2025

uniformity is stillness. it's the zero point. it's rest. but life... life is real, breathing, moving, thinking — life only emerges when the field is disturbed.

non uniformity is difference. and that difference is what makes everything possible. it had to happen. it was the ultimate plan all along. there never could be us without chaos.

separation. light from darkness. waters above from waters below. good from evil. it's what allowed creation to move. and in that motion, in that patterned non-uniformity, choice emerged. free will began.

free will is not chaos. it's divergent choices made in a coherent system... but within that freedom lies the capacity to turn away — to resist the One the story was always about. and when that happens, the resonance shifts. the pattern breaks.

you see, sin isn't just doing wrong. it's disrupting the coherence. it's introducing destructive interference... chaos — the wrong kind of divergence.

and yet the possibility of that existing was because the field was free to move in the first place. without that... there would be no fall.

there would be no redemption.

uniformity is equilibrium. non uniformity is life.
